Our Story

Started with one oven and a produce stand.

Ember & Salt opened in 2019 as a fourteen-seat counter built around a single wood-fired oven. Seven years later, the oven is still the whole point — the menu changes with what the fire and the farmers' market give us that week.

Chef Dana Reyes leads a small kitchen that sources from six farms within forty miles, and the wine list is built to match — mostly small producers, mostly things you haven't heard of yet.
